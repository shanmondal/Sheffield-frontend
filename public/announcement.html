PRESS RELEASE  ·  STRATEGIC ACQUISITION

Maco Expands Operation in Europe with Strategic Acquisition of Sheffield Industrial Saws

Maco Corporation UK Ltd, part of MACO India, has completed the strategic acquisition of the intellectual property rights, brand, goodwill and operating business assets associated with Sheffield Industrial Saws (SIS), well known manufacturer in United Kingdom of precision circular saw blades & machines for steel section rolling mills.
     India  ·  United Kingdom OFFICIAL ANNOUNCEMENT
 

Going forward, the Sheffield Industrial Saws brand will operate under Maco Corporation UK Ltd, combining Sheffield’s engineering and manufacturing expertise with Maco’s international operational platform and regional market presence.

STRATEGIC EXPANSION

This acquisition represents a significant milestone in Maco’s long-term growth strategy in global steel and engineering sector. For over five decades, Maco is doing business with steel plants across India, MENA Region and Southeast Asia with specialized engineering products & services.

PRODUCT PORTFOLIO

The expanded portfolio includes Hotsaw Blades, Friction Saw Blades, Tungsten Carbide Tipped (TCT) Blades, Segmental Blades, High-Speed Steel (HSS) Blades and Bandsaw Blades as well as Blade Grinding and hardening machines for steel mills globally.

CUSTOMER VALUE

Customers will benefit from a more integrated and responsive operating platform through the combination of Sheffield’s product expertise with Maco’s global distribution network, regional operational infrastructure and technical support.

CONTINUITY AND FUTURE OUTLOOK

The Sheffield Industrial Saws brand (SIS) and related operations will continue to be offered through the existing manufacturing infrastructure and technical teams in Sheffield, United Kingdom.

“Our objective is to preserve the engineering quality, technical expertise and reputation associated with Sheffield Industrial Saws while strengthening global reach, operational support and long-term customer value through Maco’s international platform.”

— Nagesh Patel  ·  Managing Director, Maco Corporation

⚖  Important Legal Notice — Outstanding Matters

Please be advised that Maco Corporation UK Ltd has acquired the Sheffield Industrial Saws brand, related assets and operations directly from the Liquidator of Sheffield Industrial Saws Ltd (currently in liquidation). This was strictly an acquisition of brand, assets and related operations — no obligations, receivables or liabilities of Sheffield Industrial Saws Ltd have been assumed or transferred to Maco Corporation UK Ltd.

Maco Corporation UK Ltd is a wholly independent legal entity from Sheffield Industrial Saws Ltd, which remains under liquidation. Any and all outstanding matters concerning Sheffield Industrial Saws Ltd — including unpaid invoices, claims, contractual disputes or any other liabilities — fall solely within the remit of the Liquidator.
